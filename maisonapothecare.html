About the Project —

We have worked with Maison Apothecare on their branding and packaging for over 2 years, and have been pleased to watch them grow and grow alongside them. We feel our packaging has made a difference — founder Natacha Rey has had much success in the wholesale realm selling to department & grocery stores, and this past summer opened the first Maison Apothecare brick-and-mortar shop in Niagara-on-the-Lake.

Our ongoing work for them includes packaging design, signage, print materials, & web design and maintenance, across Maison Apothecare and all it's sub brands — Sleepy Owl Baby, Gentleman's Apothecary, Maison Apothecare, Marulagold, and Lemon Aide.
